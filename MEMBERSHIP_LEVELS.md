# GEA Membership Levels

Complete inventory of all membership categories and levels. Used in Membership Levels tab of GEA Member Directory spreadsheet.

---

## Full Membership

### full_indiv - Full Membership (Individual)
- **Household Type:** Individual
- **Annual Dues (USD):** $50.00
- **Annual Dues (BWP):** P700.00
- **Voting Rights:** Yes
- **Office Eligible:** Yes
- **Eligibility:** US citizens & resident aliens who are USG employees assigned to Gaborone
- **Active:** Yes

### full_family - Full Membership (Family)
- **Household Type:** Family
- **Annual Dues (USD):** $100.00
- **Annual Dues (BWP):** P1,400.00
- **Voting Rights:** Yes
- **Office Eligible:** Yes
- **Eligibility:** US citizens & resident aliens who are USG employees + eligible family (spouse and children) assigned to Gaborone
- **Active:** Yes

## Sponsor Requirements

### Who Needs a Sponsor?
- **Affiliate Members:** YES (Full member sponsor required)
- **Associate Members:** YES (Full member sponsor required)
- **Diplomatic Members:** YES (Full member sponsor required)
- **Community Members:** YES (Full member sponsor required)
- **Full Members:** NO (Direct-hire USG employees, no sponsor needed)
- **Temporary Members:** NO (Short-term official visitors, no sponsor needed)

### Sponsor Qualifications
- Sponsor must be a **paid, active Full member** (full_indiv or full_family)
- Sponsor must have membership dues paid in full for current year
- Sponsor must have voting rights (voting status = Yes)
- Sponsor designation is optional but recommended for verification purposes

### Sponsor Role
- Sponsors vouch for the eligibility and good standing of the applicant
- Sponsors do NOT financially underwrite the membership
- Sponsors provide accountability for sponsored member conduct
- Typically an embassy colleague or senior colleague
