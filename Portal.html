Eligibility Screening

Please answer the following questions to determine your membership category.

Q1: Are you a U.S. Direct-Hire employee of the United States Government?

This includes Department of State and other USG departments and agencies.

Q1b: Are you in Botswana on temporary duty or as an official visitor?

Temporary duty = short-term assignment (typically 6 months or less). Permanent assignment = assigned to the Botswana mission as your duty station.

Q2: Are you a direct employee of the U.S. Embassy in Gaborone recruited from OUTSIDE Botswana? OR are you a U.S. citizen employed by a USG-funded contractor or implementing USG-funded programs?

Examples: Embassy staff hired from abroad, US contractor, NGO implementing US-funded programs.

⚠️ Important: For Associate membership, your position must be funded at least 51% by the U.S. Government. If your funding is less than 51% USG, you would qualify as a Community member instead. USG funding status is subject to annual confirmation.

Q3: Are you a direct employee of the U.S. Embassy in Gaborone and were recruited IN Botswana?

This includes LES (locally employed staff) and TCN (third-country nationals) hired locally in Botswana.

Q4: Are you a registered diplomat of another diplomatic or international-organization mission in Botswana?

Examples: Embassy/consulate staff with diplomatic passport, UN/African Union/SADC staff.
