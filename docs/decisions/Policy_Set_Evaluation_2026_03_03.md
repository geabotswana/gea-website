## Board Decisions - Status

### ✅ RESOLVED: Exchange Rate Determination

**Policy:** GEA Payment Policy  
**Decision:** Fixed monthly exchange rate published on the first business day of each month  
**Details:**
- Applies to all BWP conversions (EFT, cash) for that month
- Published via email to board@geabotswana.org and member portal
- Simple to implement, fair to all members, no daily fluctuations
- Easy for future non-technical Treasurers

**Status:** Implemented in GEA_Payment_Policy.docx v1.1 ✅

---

### ✅ RESOLVED: Whole Facility Booking Options

**Policy:** GEA Reservation Policy  
**Decision:** No whole facility reservations; members book court and leobo separately  
**Details:**
- Tennis/Basketball: Up to 2 hours, 3 hrs/week household limit, auto-approve if within limits
- Leobo: Up to 6 hours, 1 booking/month household limit, requires Mgmt→Board approval
- Members wanting both: Submit two separate bookings
- Simplifies operational logic (no complex two-facility blocking)
- Eliminates need to define Whole Facility-specific limits
- Each facility retains its own approval process and limits

**Status:** Implemented in GEA_Reservation_Policy.docx v1.1 ✅
