# Facility Reservation Quick Card

**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

One-page quick reference for facility booking rules, limits, and cancellation policy.

---

## Facility Limits & Rules

| Facility | Booking Limit | Time Limit | Approval | Availability |
|----------|---------------|-----------|----------|--------------|
| **Tennis Court** | 3 hrs/week | 2 hrs/session | Auto if under limit<br>Board if exceed | Walk-in + reservation |
| **Leobo** | 1/month | 6 hrs/session | Board + Management approval | Reservation only |
| **Gym** | Unlimited | No limit | Auto-approved | Walk-in + reservation |
| **Playground** | Unlimited | No limit | Auto-approved | Walk-in + reservation |
| **Basketball** | Unlimited | No limit | Auto-approved | Walk-in + reservation |

---

## How to Book

1. **Portal:** Member Portal → Reservations → [Book Facility]
2. **Facility:** Select from dropdown
3. **Date:** Pick future date (can't book past dates)
4. **Time:** Start time → End time (system calculates duration)
5. **Guest count:** Number of people attending
6. **Event name (optional):** What's the reservation for
7. **[Book Facility]** button → Submitted!

---

## Booking Status

**Green [APPROVED]** → Confirmed, you can use facility  
**Yellow [TENTATIVE]** → Waiting for board approval (usually 2-3 days)  
**Red [DENIED]** → Board denied your request (can't use)

**Leobo:** See "This month: X bookings used"  
→ Helps you check if you can book again this month

## Cancellation & Refunds

| When You Cancel | Refund |
|-----------------|--------|
| 24+ hours before | Full refund |
| 1-24 hours before | 50% refund |
| Less than 1 hour | No refund |
| No-show | No refund |

**How to cancel:** Reservations page → Click reservation → [Cancel] button

**Refund timing:** Processed within 5-7 business days

---

## Approval Timeline

| Facility | Status | Approval Time |
|----------|--------|---------------|
| Tennis (under 3 hrs/week) | [APPROVED] immediately | Same day |
| Tennis (would exceed limit) | [TENTATIVE] | 2-3 business days |
| Leobo | [TENTATIVE] | 2-3 business days (needs 2 approvals) |
| Gym/Playground/Basketball | [APPROVED] immediately | Same day |

## Special Rules

**Tennis Court:**
- Only 3 hours per week (Mon-Sun)
- Only 2 hours per booking session
- Week resets Monday at 2:00 AM

**Leobo:**
- Only 1 booking per calendar month
- Requires both Management & Board approval
- Month resets on 1st at 2:00 AM

**Gym/Playground/Basketball:**
- Walk-in access anytime
- No reservation needed
- Booking optional (for planning)

---

## Guest Lists

**Who needs to submit:** Anyone booking Tennis or Leobo with guests

**What to include:** Each guest's name, relationship, age group

**When:** Submit before 2 days before event

**RSO review:** Security check for Embassy coordination

---

## What Happens If...

**I book but don't show up?**  
→ No refund. Prevents blocking others from booking.

**Someone books the time I wanted?**  
→ That's first-come-first-served. Try different time/date.

**The facility becomes unavailable?**  
→ Board will email you. Refund or reschedule offered.

**I need to change my booking?**  
→ Cancel (get refund if >24 hrs) and book new time.

**I want to book same time every week?**  
→ Not supported in portal. Contact board to discuss recurring bookings.
