## Overview

### What is GEA?

The Gaborone Employee Association is a community organization serving U.S. Government employees, contractors, diplomats, and community members in Botswana. GEA provides access to shared facilities including a tennis court, a basketball court, a covered meeting area (the Leobo), gym facilities, and a playground.

### Why Become a Member?

As a GEA member, you enjoy:
- **Facility Access:** Book tennis court, basketball court, and Leobo; access gym and playground
- **Community:** Connect with fellow expatriates and local community members
- **Events:** Participate in organized social and recreational activities
- **Family Benefits:** Extend membership to spouse, children, and household staff
- **Membership Card:** Digital card with family member information

### Who Can Join?

GEA offers six membership categories for different groups. Read the [Membership Categories](#membership-categories) section to find the right fit.

## Membership Categories

GEA membership is determined by your employment status or official capacity. Choose the category that matches your situation. *(Hint: Answer the eligibility questions during application—the system will suggest your category.)*

### 1. **Full Membership** ($50 individual / $100 family)
**Who qualifies:** U.S. Government direct-hire employees

**You qualify if:** You are a direct employee of the U.S. Embassy or U.S. Government agency.

**Sponsorship:** Not required.

**Benefits:** Full access to all facilities; voting rights for members 17+; board eligibility for members 17+.

---

### 2. **Associate Membership** ($50 individual / $100 family)
**Who qualifies:** U.S. Government-funded employees and contractors

**You qualify if:** You are employed by a USG-funded contractor, grantee, or funded organization. (Not an employee of the U.S. Government directly, but funded by U.S. Government programs.)

**Sponsorship:** Not required.

**Benefits:** Full access to all facilities per current by-laws.

---

### 3. **Affiliate Membership** ($50 individual / $100 family)
**Who qualifies:** Embassy locally-hired employees

**You qualify if:** You are hired locally (in Botswana) by the U.S. Embassy, regardless of your citizenship.

**Sponsorship:** Not required.

**Benefits:** Full access to all facilities per current by-laws.

---

### 4. **Diplomatic Membership** ($75 individual / $150 family)
**Who qualifies:** Official diplomats and international organization personnel

**You qualify if:** You have official diplomatic status (with diplomatic passport) or are accredited personnel of an international organization headquartered in Gaborone.

**Sponsorship:** Not required.

**Benefits:** Full facility access per current by-laws.

---

### 5. **Community/Guest Membership** ($75 individual / $150 family)
**Who qualifies:** Non-government individuals in the local community

**You qualify if:** You don't fit the categories above and are part of the local Gaborone community.

**Sponsorship:** **REQUIRED**—You must be sponsored by an active Full member. The sponsor confirms you are known to the community and in good standing.

**Note:** Sponsorship is a community introduction only. Sponsors have **no financial or legal responsibility** for your membership.

**Benefits:** Full access to all facilities per current by-laws.

---

### 6. **Temporary Membership** ($20 for up to 6 months)
**Who qualifies:** U.S. Government employees on temporary duty (TDY) or temporary assignment

**You qualify if:** You are a U.S. Government employee assigned to Botswana for a limited time (temporary duty, TDY orders, or assignment letter).

**Sponsorship:** Not required.

**Benefits:** Access while in-country; membership expires after 6 months or upon departure.

**Note:** You are free to renew after your TDY ends (new application, likely to a different category).

---

### Sponsorship Questions?

**Q: Do I need a sponsor?**
Only **Community members require a sponsor**. All other categories do not.

**Q: What does my sponsor need to do?**
The sponsor simply confirms that you are known to them and in good standing in the community. No ongoing obligations—sponsorship is a one-time verification during your application.

**Q: Can anyone be a sponsor?**
Only an active Full member can sponsor you.

## Before You Apply

### Check Your Eligibility

Before starting your application, confirm:
1. **Your employment status or official capacity** matches one of the six categories above
2. **Your sponsor** (Community members only) — If you're applying for Community membership, find an active GEA Full member willing to sponsor you
3. **You have your supporting documents**, per the following section

### Gather Required Documents

Different membership categories require different documents. You'll upload these during your application:

- **Passport** — Anyone may provide a passport; diplomatic passports are preferred for those who have them
- **Omang (Botswana ID)** — Botswana citizens may provide Omang
- **Diplomatic Passport** — Required for Diplomatic category members only
- **Passport-style Photo** — 600x600–1200x1200 pixels, clear face, 54KB–10MB file
- **Employment Verification** *(if requested during application)* — Letter from employer confirming status

### Membership Year & Cost

- **Membership Year:** August 1 – July 31 (annual cycle)
- **Join Anytime:** If you join mid-year (e.g., December), you pay a pro-rated amount and your membership remains active through July 31 of the next year.
- **Example:** Join in January → Pay 75% of annual dues (Q2 rate) → Active until the next July 31
- **Payment Method:** PayPal, SDFCU transfer, Zelle, or Absa bank transfer
- **Payment Deadline:** Membership is not active until the treasurer verifies your payment

## Step-by-Step Application Process

### Timeline

**Expected Duration:** ~8 business days from application to approval (if documents are complete)

- **Board Review:** 3 business days
- **Security Officer Review:** 5 business days
- **Treasurer Verification:** 2 business days

*Timeline assumes complete documentation. If documents are missing or rejected, the timeline restarts.*

---

### Step 1: Start Your Application

1. Go to **https://geabotswana.org**
2. Click **"Member Login"** or **"New Member Application"**
3. Select **"New Member Application"** if you don't have a GEA account yet

You'll see a brief questionnaire to help determine your membership category.

---

### Step 2: Answer Eligibility Questions

The system will ask a series of yes/no questions to determine your category:

**Question 1:** Are you a U.S. Direct-Hire employee of the United States Government?
- **Yes** → Continue to next question
- **No** → Continue to Question 2

*Continuing from "Yes":**
- **Question 1b:** Are you in Botswana on temporary duty or as an official visitor?
  - **Yes** → **TEMPORARY MEMBERSHIP** selected ✓
  - **No** → **FULL MEMBERSHIP** selected ✓

*Continuing from "No" in Question 1:*

- **Question 2:** Are you a direct employee of the U.S. Embassy (recruited from outside Botswana) OR a U.S. citizen employed by a USG-funded contractor?
  - **Yes** → **ASSOCIATE MEMBERSHIP** selected ✓
  - **No** → Continue to Question 3

- **Question 3:** Are you a direct employee of the U.S. Embassy (recruited in Botswana, i.e., local hire)?
  - **Yes** → **AFFILIATE MEMBERSHIP** selected ✓
  - **No** → Continue to Question 4

- **Question 4:** Are you a registered diplomat of another diplomatic or international-organization mission in Botswana?
  - **Yes** → **DIPLOMATIC MEMBERSHIP** selected ✓
  - **No** → **COMMUNITY MEMBERSHIP** selected ✓

The system will select your category based on your answers.

---

### Step 3: Enter Personal Information

You'll fill out a form with your details. What you enter depends on whether you're applying as an individual or family.

#### **Individual Applicant:**
- **First Name & Last Name**
- **Email** (you'll log in with this email and a temporary password)
- **Phone:** Country code + phone number (e.g., +267 for Botswana)
- **WhatsApp Enabled?** (checkbox to indicate if your phone number is WhatsApp-enabled)
- **Employer/Organization** *(if required for your category)*
- **Job Title** (e.g., "Program Officer", "Teacher", "Diplomat")
- **Starting Date** *(if required for your category; estimate if not known exactly)*
- **Anticipated Departure Date** *(if required for your category; estimate if not known exactly)*
- **Sponsor Information** *(if required for your category)* — Full name and email of your GEA Full member sponsor

#### **Family Applicant:**
- **Primary Member Info** (as above—usually the primary earner)
- **Spouse Info** — First name, last name, phone (optional)
- **Children** — For each child:
  - First name, last name
  - Date of birth (required; determines some membership privileges)
- **Household Staff** *(optional)* — If applicable:
  - Name, phone
  - Passport or Omang number
  - Employment start date

---

### Step 4: Confirm Application

Review your information and click **"Submit Application"**.

You'll receive:
1. **Welcome Email** with a temporary password
2. **Instructions** to log into the member portal
3. **Next Steps:** Upload required documents

---

### Step 5: Log In & Upload Documents

1. Log in to your member portal with your email and temporary password
2. *(Optional)* You can change your password anytime using the password reset link
3. Navigate to **"My Application"** section
4. You'll see which documents are **required** for your category:

   - **Passport** — For non-citizens or citizens applying for some categories
   - **Omang (Botswana ID)** — For citizens, if applicable
   - **Diplomatic Passport** — Only for Diplomatic members
   - **Passport-style Photo** — Required for all applicants

5. **Upload each required document:**
   - Click **"Upload"** for each document type
   - Select the file from your computer (PDF, JPG, or PNG)
   - Confirm upload

**Document Requirements:**
- **Photo:** 600x600–1200x1200 pixels, clear face shot, any background, 54KB–10MB
- **Passport/Omang/Documents:** Clear, legible, full-page scans (PDF or high-quality JPG/PNG)
- **File Size:** 10MB maximum per file

**Resubmit:** If you need to replace a document, simply upload a new file—it will overwrite the previous one.

---

### Step 6: Board Reviews Your Application

The GEA Board will review your application and documents within **3 business days**.

The Board checks:
- ✓ All required documents submitted?
- ✓ Application information complete?
- ✓ Sponsor verified? *(if required for your category)*
- ✓ Any concerns or questions?

**Possible Outcomes:**

**A) Approved** ✓
- Your documents are sent to the Security Officer (RSO) for review
- You'll receive an email: *"Your application is being reviewed by our security officer"*
- **Next Step:** Wait for RSO approval (typically 5 business days)

**B) Need More Information**
- Board requests clarification or additional info
- You'll receive an email with specific details needed
- **Next Step:** Log in and provide the requested information

**C) Denied**
- Board decides not to approve your application
- You'll receive an email with the reason (e.g., "Invalid sponsor", "Incomplete application")
- **Next Step:** You cannot reapply on this email without Board approval. Contact the board at board@geabotswana.org to discuss.

---

### Step 7: Security Officer Reviews Documents

If your application is approved by the Board, the Security Officer (RSO) will review your documents within **5 business days**.

The RSO checks:
- ✓ Passport/Omang valid and current?
- ✓ Diplomatic passport correct? *(Diplomatic members only)*
- ✓ Photo acceptable quality?
- ✓ Employment verification valid? *(if applicable)*

**Possible Outcomes:**

**A) All Documents Approved** ✓
- You'll receive an email: *"Your documents are approved! Next, submit payment."*
- **Next Step:** Proceed to Step 8 (Payment)

**B) Documents Rejected**
- RSO rejected one or more documents (e.g., "Passport expired", "Photo unclear")
- You'll receive an email with specific reasons and a deadline to resubmit (typically 10 business days)
- **Next Step:** Log in, upload replacement documents, and resubmit

---

### Step 8: Submit Payment Proof

Once your documents are approved by the RSO, you're ready to pay membership dues.

**Payment Amount:**
- Depends on your membership category and household type (individual vs. family)
- See [Payment Information](#payment-information) section below for amounts
- Pro-rated if joining mid-year

**How to Pay:**
The portal provides instructions for four payment methods:
1. **PayPal** — Fastest and most convenient (geabotswana.org@gmail.com or linked Stripe)
2. **SDFCU Transfer** — Bank-to-bank transfer to SDFCU account
3. **Zelle** — U.S.-based money transfer (if available in your region)
4. **Absa Bank** — Direct deposit to Absa account in Botswana

**Submit Payment Proof:**
1. Make your payment using one of the methods above
2. Save your **payment receipt or confirmation** (screenshot or PDF)
3. Log into your member portal
4. Go to **"Submit Payment Proof"**
5. Upload your receipt
6. Click **"Submit"**

You'll receive an email confirmation: *"Payment received. We'll verify within 2 business days."*

---

### Step 9: Treasurer Verifies Payment

The GEA Treasurer will verify your payment within **2 business days**.

**What They're Checking:**
- ✓ Payment amount matches membership dues?
- ✓ Payment method acceptable?
- ✓ Receipt is genuine?

**Possible Outcomes:**

**A) Payment Verified** ✓
- Treasurer confirms your payment
- **Congratulations!** Your membership is **ACTIVE**
- You'll receive an email: *"Welcome to GEA! Your membership is now active."*
- **Next Step:** Proceed to Step 10 (Unlock Full Portal Access)

**B) Payment Issue**
- Treasurer needs clarification (e.g., "Amount unclear from receipt")
- You'll receive an email with specific questions
- **Next Step:** Reply to the email with requested information

**C) Payment Not Verified**
- Treasurer cannot verify your payment (e.g., wrong amount, invalid method)
- You'll receive an email asking you to resubmit payment proof
- **Next Step:** Make another payment and resubmit proof

---

### Step 10: Your Membership is Active! 🎉

Once the Treasurer verifies your payment, your GEA membership is **fully active**.

You now have access to:
- **📅 Reservations:** Book tennis court, basketball court, and Leobo; access gym and playground
- **👥 Member Directory:** View other GEA members and connect
- **🏠 Family Management:** View all household members
- **📝 My Profile:** Update contact info, add additional photos
- **🪪 Membership Card:** Digital card showing your status and expiration date
- **📧 Member Communications:** Receive event updates, facility alerts, and news

### Membership Expiration

Your membership is active through **July 31** of the current membership year, regardless of when you joined.

**Example:** Join in December 2025 → Active through July 31, 2026 → Renewal application opens June 2026.

**Renewal:** You'll receive renewal reminders 30 days and 7 days before expiration. Renewal follows the same process (brief questionnaire, document verification if needed, payment).

## Timeline & Status Tracking

### Application Timeline

```
Day 1:          Submit Application
                ↓
Days 2–4:       Board Review (3 business days)
                ↓ (if approved)
Days 5–9:       Security Officer Review (5 business days)
                ↓ (if approved)
Day 10:         Payment Submission
                ↓
Days 11–12:     Treasurer Verification (2 business days)
                ↓
Day 13:         ✓ Membership Active!
```

**Typical Duration:** 8–13 business days (if all documents are complete)

**What Can Cause Delays?**
- Missing documents → RSO/Board requests resubmission → Timeline restarts
- Unclear photo → Need new photo upload
- Payment issue → Need payment clarification → Timeline adds 2–3 days
- Weekends/holidays → Processing pauses

### Check Your Status

1. Log into your member portal (https://geabotswana.org)
2. Go to **"My Application"**
3. You'll see your current status, next steps, and any pending items

**Status Indicators:**
- 🟡 **Submitted** — Awaiting Board review
- 🟡 **Documents Sent to Security Review** — Board approved; RSO reviewing
- 🟡 **Documents Approved, Awaiting Payment** — RSO approved; need to submit payment
- 🟡 **Payment Submitted** — Awaiting Treasurer verification
- 🟢 **Active** — Membership is live! ✓
- 🔴 **Denied** — Application not approved (check email for reason)
- 🟡 **More Information Needed** — Board/Treasurer needs clarification (check email)

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Who should I contact if I have questions during my application?**
A: Email board@geabotswana.org. The GEA board will respond within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I apply if I'm not currently in Botswana?**
A: Yes! You can apply remotely and upload documents electronically. Membership becomes active when payment is verified, and you can use facilities once you arrive.

**Q: How long is my membership valid?**
A: Membership is valid through July 31 of the current membership year. If you join mid-year, your membership extends to July 31 of the next year. You can renew annually.

**Q: What if my employment status changes during my membership?**
A: Contact the board at board@geabotswana.org. Your membership category may need to be updated. (For example, if you transition from Full to Associate, your dues may change.)

**Q: Can I add family members after I join?**
A: Currently, family members must be included in your initial application. If you need to add family members later, contact the board to discuss options.

**Q: What if I lose my temporary password?**
A: Click **"Forgot Password"** on the login screen, and a reset link will be emailed to you.

---

### Category & Sponsorship Questions

**Q: What if I don't qualify for any category?**
A: You likely qualify for Community membership. Confirm that you meet the Community criteria (non-government individual in the local community) and find a Full member to sponsor you.

**Q: My sponsor is a Full member but their membership expires soon. Can they still sponsor me?**
A: Yes, your sponsor must be an **active** Full member at the time of your application. Once your application is submitted, your sponsor's status doesn't affect your approval.

**Q: Can I choose a different category if I qualify for multiple?**
A: Yes! During the application, if you qualify for multiple categories, you can select your preference. Choose the category that best fits your situation.

**Q: I'm an Associate member. Can I sponsor someone?**
A: No, only Full members can sponsor. To be clear: Community members are the only ones who need sponsors—only Full members can provide that sponsorship.

**Q: What if my sponsor doesn't respond to board requests?**
A: The board will contact your sponsor. If unable to verify sponsorship, your application may be denied. Contact the board for updates.

---

### Document Questions

**Q: My passport is expiring soon. Can I still submit it?**
A: Yes, but your passport must be valid at the time of submission. If it expires before your application is approved, you'll need to resubmit a new valid passport.

**Q: Can I submit a photocopy instead of a scan?**
A: No, the system requires digital uploads (PDF, JPG, PNG). Scan or photograph your documents clearly.

**Q: What if my photo doesn't meet specifications?**
A: The system will tell you if your photo is too small, too large, or unclear. You can easily replace it and resubmit. Make sure your face is clearly visible.

**Q: Do I need an apostille or certified copy of my documents?**
A: No, standard scans are sufficient for the initial submission. RSO may request certified copies if needed.

**Q: What if I don't have a passport or Omang?**
A: You must have valid identification. If you're missing required documents, contact the board about alternatives.

---

### Payment Questions

**Q: What if I pay but forget to upload my receipt?**
A: Your membership will not be activated until you submit proof of payment. Upload the receipt as soon as possible. If the Treasurer can't verify your payment within 2 business days, they'll send you a reminder.

**Q: Can I pay in Botswana Pula instead of USD?**
A: Yes! The portal displays the equivalent amount in BWP using daily exchange rates. You can pay in either currency using the appropriate payment method (SDFCU/Absa for BWP, PayPal/Zelle for USD).

**Q: What if I paid but the Treasurer says they don't see my payment?**
A: Sometimes payments take time to process. Check:
1. Did you use the correct account/email?
2. Did you include your name and household ID in the memo?
3. Is your receipt showing the right amount?
Submit your receipt again, and the Treasurer will investigate.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I change my mind?**
A: Refund policy is determined by the GEA Board. Contact board@geabotswana.org to discuss.

**Q: Is there a payment deadline?**
A: No fixed deadline after submission, but membership doesn't become active until the Treasurer verifies your payment. The sooner you submit proof, the sooner you can use facilities.

---

### Status & Timeline Questions

**Q: Why is my application taking longer than expected?**
A: Most delays are due to:
- Missing or unclear documents (Board/RSO will email you)
- Weekend/holiday pauses (no processing on weekends)
- Payment issue (Treasurer needs clarification)
- High volume of applications (rare, but possible)

Check your portal for status and any pending requests.

**Q: Can I check my application status online?**
A: Yes! Log in to your member portal and go to **"My Application"**. You'll see your current status and next steps.

**Q: How do I know when the Board has reviewed my application?**
A: You'll receive an email when the Board makes a decision (Approved / Denied / Need More Info). You can also log in to check your status anytime.

**Q: What if I don't receive an email notification?**
A: Check your spam folder. If you still don't see it, log in to your portal to check your status. Contact board@geabotswana.org if you need help.

---

### After Membership Activation

**Q: What can I do now that I'm a member?**
A: You can:
- Book tennis court, basketball court, and Leobo; access gym and playground
- View the member directory and connect with others
- Edit your profile and add photos
- Download your digital membership card
- Participate in GEA events

**Q: Do I have voting rights?**
A: Voting rights are available to Full members who are 17 years old or older. Other categories do not have voting rights.

**Q: Can I be on the Board?**
A: Board eligibility is limited to Full members who are 17 years old or older. Contact the board to express interest.

**Q: How do I renew my membership?**
A: In late June/early July (before July 31 expiration), you'll receive an email with renewal instructions. The renewal process is similar to the initial application but typically faster if your documents are still valid.

**Q: What if my membership expires?**
A: You'll receive reminder emails at 30 days and 7 days before expiration. After July 31, your membership becomes inactive and you lose facility access. You'll need to submit a renewal application (or new application) to reactivate.

## Appendix: Common Terms

**Active Member:** A member whose payment has been verified by the Treasurer and whose membership is currently valid through July 31.

**Board:** The GEA Board of Directors, which reviews applications, approves memberships, and manages facility policies.

**Facility:** A shared recreational resource available to GEA members (tennis court, basketball, Leobo, gym, playground).

**Household:** A membership unit consisting of a primary member and any spouse, children, or household staff.

**Household ID:** A unique identifier assigned to each membership household (format: HHS-YYYY-MM-DD-###).

**RSO (Regional Security Officer):** A security professional who reviews applicant documents (passports, Omang, photos) to confirm authenticity and validity.

**Sponsor:** An active Full member who verifies that you are known to the community and in good standing. Required only for Community membership.

**Membership Year:** The annual membership period, August 1 – July 31.

**Treasurer:** The board member responsible for verifying payment and activating memberships.

**Verification Status:** The current state of your application (Submitted, Board Review, RSO Review, Awaiting Payment, Activated, Denied).
