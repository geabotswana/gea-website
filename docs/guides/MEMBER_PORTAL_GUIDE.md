# Member Portal Guide

**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

Complete step-by-step guide for GEA members using the member portal to manage memberships, reservations, and payments.

---

## Getting Started

### First Time Login

1. **Visit the portal:** https://geabotswana.org/member.html
2. **Click "Admin Login"** at the top (yes, it says "Admin" — this button serves both members and admins)
3. **Enter your email and password**
4. **Click "Login"**
5. **You'll see your dashboard**

Your login email is the one on record with GEA. If you don't remember your password, contact board@geabotswana.org.

### Session Security

- Your session stays active for 24 hours
- Closing your browser **does not** log you out (but refreshing after 24 hours will)
- When in doubt, look for **Logout** button in top-right corner
- **Always log out** when finished, especially on shared computers

## Reservations (Book & Manage Facilities)

**Location:** Member Portal → Reservations

Your central hub for booking, managing, and viewing facility reservations.

### Your Current Reservations

**Section:** "Current Reservations"

Lists all your active and pending bookings:

- **Facility:** Which facility (Tennis Court, Leobo, etc.)
- **Date:** When you booked it
- **Time:** What time your reservation is
- **Status badge:**
  - [APPROVED] — Confirmed, you can use the facility
  - [TENTATIVE] — Waiting to be confirmed (will notify you when confirmed)
  - [DENIED] — Not approved; board denied your request
- **Cancel button:** If APPROVED, you can cancel anytime (with refund details shown)

**What each status means:**

| Status | Meaning | What to Do |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **APPROVED** | Confirmed; you can use facility on your date/time | Show up at scheduled time |
| **TENTATIVE** | Waiting to see if higher-priority booking gets cancelled; you'll be auto-promoted if it does | Monitor for confirmation email |
| **DENIED** | Board declined your booking; reason shown in detail pane | Request different date or contact board@geabotswana.org if you have questions |

### Cancelling a Reservation

If you need to cancel:

1. Click your reservation
2. Detail pane shows cancel button and refund terms
3. [Cancel] button → Confirm cancellation
4. Confirmation email sent
5. Refund processed (amount depends on facility and cancellation timing)

### Book a New Facility

**Section:** "Book New Facility"

1. **Choose your facility:**
   - Dropdown menu lists: Tennis Court, Leobo, Gym, Basketball, Playground

2. **Pick a date:**
   - Calendar shows future dates (can't book past dates)
   - Unavailable dates appear grayed out

3. **Select your time:**
   - Start time: When you want to begin
   - End time: When you want to finish
   - Duration automatically calculates
   - System prevents booking past facility closing time

4. **Guest count:**
   - How many people attending (including yourself)
   - Used for capacity tracking and event coordination

5. **Event name/purpose:**
   - What's this reservation for? (e.g., "Family tennis game," "Birthday party," "Basketball league")
   - Optional but helpful for board coordination

6. **[Book Facility] button:**
   - System checks your usage limits (see below)
   - If under limit: You'll see "APPROVED" immediately
   - If over limit: You'll see "TENTATIVE" — board will review

### Facility Limits (Important!)

**Tennis Court:**
- Maximum: 3 hours per week
- Maximum per session: 2 hours
- How it works: If you book less than 3 hours/week, it auto-approves; if you'd exceed 3, it goes to board for approval

**Leobo:**
- Maximum: 1 booking per month
- How it works: Always requires board approval (and management approval first); you'll see TENTATIVE until approved

**Gym, Playground, Basketball:**
- Unlimited walk-up
- First-come, first-served
- Auto-approves immediately

### Usage Tracking

Portal shows your current week's usage:
- Tennis Court: "Current week: 2.5 hours used" (helps you see if you can book more)
- Leobo: "This month: 0 bookings used" (helps you see if you can book)

## Profile (Your Information)

**Location:** Member Portal → Profile

Manage your personal information and documents.

### Personal Information (Read-Only)

- First name, last name
- Date of birth
- Citizenship country
- US citizen status

These are set by the board during account creation; contact board@geabotswana.org if changes needed.

### Contact Information (Editable)

**Primary Phone:**
- Phone number
- Country code (select from dropdown)
- [WhatsApp?] checkbox (indicate if this number has WhatsApp)

**Secondary Phone:**
- Phone number (optional)
- Country code
- [WhatsApp?] checkbox

**[Save Changes] button** → Updates your contact info

### Emergency Contact (Editable)

- Emergency contact name
- Relationship to you (e.g., "Spouse," "Parent")
- Email address
- Phone number

[Save Changes] → Updates emergency contact

### Employment (If Applicable)

Shows only if your membership category requires employment verification:

- Office/employer name
- Job title
- Employment start date
- Employment verification file status (approved or pending)

If pending verification, you may be asked to upload employment document.

### Documents & Verification

**Passport:**
- Status: [VERIFIED], [PENDING], or [REJECTED]
- Expiration date (if uploaded)
- [Upload] button to upload passport photo

**Omang (National ID):**
- Status: [VERIFIED], [PENDING], or [REJECTED]
- Expiration date (if uploaded)
- [Upload] button to upload Omang photo

**Photo (Membership Card Photo):**
- Status: [APPROVED], [PENDING], or [REJECTED]
- [Upload] button to upload your photo for membership card

**Upload Document Instructions:**

1. Click [Upload] button next to document type
2. Modal appears:
   - Choose document type (Passport, Omang, or Photo)
   - [Select File] button → Pick image from your computer
   - [Upload] button
3. File uploads to system
4. Board or RSO reviews (may take a few days)
5. You'll receive email with result (approved/rejected)
6. If rejected, reason provided; you can resubmit

**Document Requirements:**
- Clear, readable image
- All text legible
- Full document visible (not cropped)
- For ID documents: Front and back if required
- For photo: Face clearly visible, professional appearance

## Payment (Track Dues & Submit Proof)

**Location:** Member Portal → Payment

Manage your annual GEA membership dues.

### Payment Methods Available

GEA accepts payment via:

**PayPal:**
- Email: gea@geabotswana.org (or check payment instructions for link)
- Easiest for international members

**SDFCU Member-to-Member:**
- Bank account details shown on this page
- For US-based account holders

**Zelle:**
- Phone number shown on this page
- Fast domestic transfer

**Absa:**
- Bank account details shown on this page
- For Botswana-based account holders

### Your Dues Information

**Membership Year:**
- Shows current membership year (e.g., "2025-2026")
- Membership years typically run August 1 to July 31

**Annual Dues:**
- Amount due for full year
- Shown in both USD and BWP
- Check your membership category for your specific amount

**Pro-Rated Dues (If Joining Mid-Year):**

If you join partway through the year, you pay a pro-rated amount:

| Quarter | Percentage | Due |
|---------|------------|-----|
| Q1 (Aug-Oct) | 100% | Full amount |
| Q2 (Nov-Jan) | 75% | Three-quarters |
| Q3 (Feb-Apr) | 50% | Half |
| Q4 (May-Jul) | 25% | One-quarter |

**Your Balance Due:**
- Shows what you still owe for the membership year
- Updates as you submit and board verifies payments

### Submit Payment Proof

**Section:** "Submit Payment Verification"

After making payment via any method above, submit proof so board can verify:

1. **[Select File]** → Choose receipt/screenshot from your computer
   - Can be: Bank confirmation email, PayPal screenshot, Zelle screenshot, bank receipt

2. **Payment Method dropdown** → Select which method you used
   - PayPal / SDFCU / Zelle / Absa / Other

3. **Amount** → How much you paid
   - Numeric value (e.g., "500" for 500 USD or 500 BWP)

4. **Currency** → USD or BWP
   - Select which currency you paid in

5. **Payment Date** → When you made the payment
   - Calendar picker

6. **[Submit Payment Verification]** button
   - Receipt uploaded and sent to board treasurer
   - Board will verify and update your account

### Payment Status

**Section:** "Submitted Payments"

Shows all payments you've submitted with their status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Submitted** | Received; board is reviewing |
| **Verified** | Approved! Your account updated |
| **Rejected** | Board couldn't verify; may have asked for clarification |
| **Clarification Requested** | Board needs more info from you (check email) |

**For each payment:**
- Amount and currency shown
- Date submitted
- Verification date (if approved)
- Reason (if rejected or needs clarification)

**If rejected or needs clarification:**
- Check your email for details
- Address the issue (resubmit receipt, provide more info, etc.)
- Resubmit payment proof with corrections

- Visual 11-step progress bar showing where you are

- If board approved you, payment submission instructions

- All actions taken on your application

### What You Need to Do

The page shows **"Next Step"** clearly:

- **"Submit Documents"** → Upload your passport/omang to the Profile page

- **"Submit Payment"** → Go to Payment page, follow instructions
- **"Payment Pending"** → Wait for treasurer to verify your payment

## Session & Security

### How Long Can I Stay Logged In?

- Your session lasts 24 hours from last activity
- Closing your browser doesn't log you out
- After 24 hours of no activity, you're automatically logged out
- Refreshing the page after 24 hours forces re-login

### Staying Secure

- **Don't share your password** with anyone
- **Log out** when finished, especially on shared computers
- **Use HTTPS** (browser shows lock icon — always check)
- **Don't write down your password** (contact board to reset if forgotten)

### Forgotten Password?

Contact board@geabotswana.org:
- Email your request with your email address on file
- Board will reset and send temporary password
- Log in and change to new password

## Common Questions

**Q: How long until my application is approved?**
A: Typical timeline is 3-4 weeks. You can see your progress on the Applicant page. Board meets monthly for final decisions.

**Q: Can I book multiple facilities at the same time?**
A: Yes, each facility has separate limits. You can have multiple active reservations.

**Q: What if I need to change my reservation time?**
A: Cancel your current reservation and book a new one. If you need help, contact board@geabotswana.org.

**Q: Do my household members have portal access?**
A: They can have accounts if you add them to your household. Contact board@geabotswana.org to set up access.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I cancel my reservation?**
A: Refund terms depend on facility and timing. Details shown when you cancel. Tennis Court refunds are typically full unless cancelled within 24 hours.

**Q: What if the system says my payment was rejected?**
A: Check email for reason. Common issues: receipt unclear, amount doesn't match, or wrong payment method indicated. Resubmit with corrected information.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Contact board@geabotswana.org with your email. They'll reset it and send you a temporary password. Change to a new password after login.
