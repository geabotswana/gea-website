# Payment Submission Quick Card

**Last Updated:** April 25, 2026

One-page reference for GEA membership dues payment methods and verification process.

---

## Your Annual Dues

**For Calendar Year:** August 1 – July 31

| Category | USD | BWP |
|----------|-----|-----|
| Full Member | $250 | P3,000 |
| Associate Member | $250 | P3,000 |
| Affiliate Member | $200 | P2,400 |
| Diplomatic Member | $200 | P2,400 |
| Community Member | $150 | P1,800 |
| Temporary Member | $62.50 (3 months) | P750 |

**Pro-ration** (if joining mid-year):
- Q1 (Aug-Oct): 100% of annual
- Q2 (Nov-Jan): 75% of annual
- Q3 (Feb-Apr): 50% of annual
- Q4 (May-Jul): 25% of annual

## Payment Methods

### **1. PayPal (Preferred for USD)**
**Email:** gea@geabotswana.org  
**Currency:** USD  
**Fee:** PayPal fee applies (usually 3-4%)  
**Best for:** International members, no bank needed

### **2. Zelle (Preferred for USD - US Only)**
**Phone Number:** [See portal Payment page]  
**Currency:** USD

**Best for:** US bank accounts, fastest option

### **3. SDFCU Member-to-Member (USD)**
**Account Details:** [See portal Payment page]  
**Currency:** USD  
**Fee:** No fee for members  
**Best for:** Federal credit union members

### **4. Absa Bank Transfer (BWP)**
**Account Details:** [See portal Payment page]  
**Currency:** BWP  
**Fee:** Minimal bank fee  
**Best for:** Botswana bank accounts  

### **5. Cash (Last Resort)**
**Payment:** Deliver to board member  
**Currency:** BWP preferred  
**Verification:** Board member must document receipt  
**Best for:** Emergency only

## How to Submit Payment Proof

**Step 1: Make Payment**  
Choose one of the methods above and complete the transfer

**Step 2: Get Proof**  
- PayPal: Take screenshot of payment confirmation
- Zelle/Bank: Take screenshot of bank confirmation
- Cash: Get receipt from board member

**Step 3: Go to Portal**  
Member Portal → Payment → [Submit Payment Verification]

**Step 4: Fill Out Form**
- Upload file (JPG/PNG/PDF, under 10 MB)
- Select payment method used
- Enter amount paid (number only, e.g., "250")
- Select currency: USD or BWP
- Select payment date
- [Submit Payment Verification]

**Step 5: Wait for Approval**  
- Status shows "Submitted"
- Board treasurer verifies (usually 3-5 business days)
- You receive email when approved

**System uses:** Current USD → BWP exchange rate (updated nightly)

**Equivalent amounts:**
- $250 USD ≈ P3,000 BWP (rate dependent)
- $200 USD ≈ P2,400 BWP (rate dependent)
- $150 USD ≈ P1,800 BWP (rate dependent)

**You can pay in either currency** as long as it equals your dues.

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Submitted** | Received, awaiting review | Wait 3-5 days |
| **Verified** | Approved! Account updated | Done! Dues paid |
| **Rejected** | Not accepted, see reason | Resubmit with corrections |
| **Clarification Requested** | Need more info | Email you with question |

## Common Payment Issues & Fixes

**"Receipt not legible"**  
→ Retake screenshot with better lighting, ensure full confirmation visible

**"Amount doesn't match"**  
→ Verify you paid correct amount for your category; pro-ration applies if mid-year

**"Currency mismatch"**  
→ Make sure currency selected matches what you paid in

**"Payment method wrong"**  
→ Select actual method used; verify you used that method

**"Still showing submitted after 1 week"**  
→ Treasurer may be busy; send friendly reminder to board@geabotswana.org

## Payment Deadline

**Membership Year:** August 1 – July 31  
**Dues Deadline:** August 31 each year  
**Late Notice:** Sent ~30 days before deadline  
**Lapsed:** Membership ends July 31 if not paid by August 31  

---

## What Happens Next

**After your payment verified:**
1. Portal updates: Dues paid
2. Email confirmation sent
3. Next year starts Aug 1
4. You'll be reminded to pay next year ~30 days before deadline

**If payment rejected:**
1. You'll receive detailed reason
2. You can resubmit corrected proof
3. Will not impact membership until deadline passes
