# **GEA COMMUNICATIONS POLICY**
*Gaborone Employee Association*
Effective: March 3, 2026

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes standards for GEA member communications, including email tone, contact methods, notification frequency, and member preferences. The policy ensures communications are professional, timely, respectful, and aligned with GEA values.
## 2. Communication Channels
### Primary Channel: Email
Email is GEA's primary communication channel. All official communications (membership updates, payment reminders, reservation confirmations, board decisions) are sent via email.
Member email addresses collected during application:
Personal email (preferred for members to maintain contact)
Work email (for workplace-related items if member provides)

Default: Communications sent to personal email. Members can update email preferences in their portal profile.
### Secondary Channels
Member portal announcements – For time-sensitive alerts
SMS (future) – Only with member consent; use for urgent facility closures
Slack/WhatsApp (future) – Only if formally adopted by Board

## 3. Email Tone & Style
All GEA emails must follow these guidelines:
### Professional Tone
Friendly and approachable, not stiff or overly formal
Clear and concise – no unnecessary jargon or complexity
Respectful of member time – get to the point quickly
Positive framing – emphasize solutions over problems

✅ GOOD: "Your payment of $50 has been verified. Your facility access is now active. Welcome to GEA!"

❌ BAD: "We have received your funds. Processing initiated. Awaiting completion of protocol."

### Signature
All official GEA emails must include:
Sender name and title (e.g., "Jane Doe, GEA Treasurer")
GEA contact email (board@geabotswana.org or treasurer@geabotswana.org)
Optional: GEA website or member portal URL
❌ Never: Personal cell phone numbers or personal emails in official communications

## 4. Types of Communications & Sending Rules
### TRANSACTIONAL (Immediate Response Required)
Sent immediately upon the triggering event:
Payment verification confirmation – sent when Treasurer verifies payment
Application status updates – sent when Board approves/denies application
Reservation confirmation – sent when reservation is approved
Facility closure notice – sent immediately if facility becomes unavailable
Requirement: Sent within same business day.
Template: Member receives confirmation of action taken and any next steps.

### OPERATIONAL (Scheduled & Routine)
Sent on regular schedule:
Dues invoices – sent August 1 (new membership year)
Dues payment reminders – sent August 15 if unpaid
Facility closure announcements – sent when planned closures scheduled
Annual calendar/events – sent once per year (August)
Requirement: Sent per schedule; at least 10 days notice for deadline-related items.
Template: Clear deadline, payment method, contact for questions.

### INFORMATIONAL (Board Decisions & Updates)
Sent to inform members of Board decisions:
Policy changes – sent when Board updates any policy
Board election results – sent after annual Board election
Member directory updates – sent if member privacy settings changed
Facility rule updates – sent when facility access or reservation rules change
Requirement: Sent within 5 business days of Board decision.
Template: Explain change, why it was made, effective date, link to full policy.

### PROMOTIONAL (Events & Special Announcements)
Sent for special events and member engagement:
Member social events – GEA picnics, holiday gatherings
Facility maintenance windows – when facilities will be unavailable
Member spotlights or community news – optional content (members can opt out)
Requirement: Minimum 3 weeks notice for scheduled events.
Template: Event details, date/time, how to RSVP, what to bring.
Note: Members can opt out of promotional emails (see Section 5).

## 5. Member Notification Preferences
Members can control what communications they receive via their portal profile:
### Required Emails (Opt-Out Not Available)
Members cannot opt out of these critical communications:
Payment verification confirmations
Membership status changes (activation, suspension, expiration)
Reservation confirmations and approvals
Facility closure notices (safety-related)
Policy changes (compliance notification)

### Optional Emails (Opt-Out Available)
Members can opt out of these communications:
Dues payment reminders (non-critical deadline notices)
Promotional emails (events, spotlights, announcements)
Annual calendar/facility updates

Process: Members manage preferences in portal under "Communication Settings". Default: all optional emails enabled.

## 6. Communication Frequency Limits
To avoid email overload, GEA limits communication frequency:
### Per Member, Per Month
Transactional emails: No limit (sent as needed)
Operational emails: Maximum 2 per month (e.g., invoice + reminder)
Informational emails: Maximum 2 per month (board updates)
Promotional emails: Maximum 2 per month
Total: No more than 6 non-transactional emails per member per month.
### Exceptions
Facility closures (safety): No limit
Payment overdue: Can send one reminder per month
Major policy changes: No limit (compliance requirement)

## 7. Response Time Standards
When members contact GEA via email:
Treasurer (treasurer@geabotswana.org): Respond within 2 business days
Board (board@geabotswana.org): Respond within 3 business days
Automated confirmations: Sent immediately (e.g., payment submission, reservation request)

If issue cannot be resolved by deadline, send holding email with timeline for full response.

## 8. Email Template Consistency
All GEA emails must use approved templates from EMAIL_TEMPLATES.md:
Do NOT create custom emails – use existing templates
Personalize with member name and relevant details
Templates approved by Board and stored in EMAIL_TEMPLATES.md
If new email type needed, get Board approval before sending

Benefit: Consistent tone, professional appearance, legal compliance.

## 9. Sensitive Information in Emails
Guidelines for handling sensitive member data:
### NEVER Include in Emails
Passwords, tokens, or login credentials
Government ID numbers or passport numbers
Payment method details (credit card, bank account numbers)
Full payment history or financial statements
Sponsor names or relationship information (privacy)
### OK to Include
Member name, email, phone (already in their profile)
Membership category, status, dues amount
Confirmation of payment received (amount, date, method name only)
Reservation details (facility, date, time, approval status)

## 10. Unsubscribe & Complaints
Every email must include:
Unsubscribe link for optional emails (dues reminders, promotional)
Contact address for complaints/feedback (treasurer@geabotswana.org)

Process for member complaints:
Member emails treasurer about communication issue
Treasurer responds within 2 business days with explanation or apology
If member requests unsubscribe from optional emails, update preferences immediately
If pattern of complaints, Board reviews communication practices

## 11. Testing & Approval
Before sending any bulk email:
Test email sent to Board member first
Verify formatting, links, member names are correct
For new email types: Get Board approval via email before sending to members
For routine emails: Treasurer has authority to send (no Board approval needed)
