# **GEA DATA MANAGEMENT POLICY**
Gaborone Employee Association
Effective: March 3, 2026
## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes what data GEA collects, how it is organized, who can access it, how it is protected, and how long it is retained. The policy ensures member privacy, data security, and compliance with applicable privacy regulations and federal guidelines (6 FAM 500-546).
## 2. What Data GEA Collects
### Member Identity & Contact Information
First and last name
Date of birth
Nationality / country of residence
Email address (personal and/or work)
Phone number(s) (personal and/or work)
Mailing address
Passport-style photo (digital)
### Employment & Sponsorship Data
Employment status / category (Full, Associate, Affiliate, Diplomatic, Temporary, Community/Guest)
Employer / organization
Position / job title
Sponsoring Full member (for non-Full categories)
Government ID number (if provided)
### Membership & Access Data
Membership status (Active, Inactive, Resigned, Denied)
Membership start date
Membership expiration / renewal date
Facility access status (Active, Suspended, Restricted)
Voting eligibility (Yes/No)
Board eligibility (Yes/No)
### Financial Data
Membership dues amount (by category)
Payment method (EFT, PayPal, Federal Credit Union, Cash)
Payment date
Payment status (Verified, Pending, Overdue, Unpaid)
Proof of payment (receipt, confirmation email)
Payment history (all previous years' payments)
### Facility Reservation & Guest Data
Reservation date, time, facility
Reservation status (Approved, Tentative, Waitlisted, Cancelled)
Guest names and dates of birth (for facilities requiring guest lists)
RSO guest approval status
### Submission Documents
Employment verification documents
Passport/ID scans
Other required documents (held per Document Submission Policy)
## 3. Data Storage & Organization
All GEA data is stored in Google Workspace on a dedicated Shared Drive, organized by category for secure access and easy retrieval.
### Google Workspace Organization
All data stored in GEA Shared Drive (Organizational shared storage)
Shared Drive persists ownership: Does not depend on individual user accounts
All active members can be added/removed from Shared Drive without disrupting access
Shared Drive automatically backed up by Google (complies with Google's enterprise backup policies)
### Folder Structure
Main Folder: GEA Management System
├── Members (Master directory & household records)
├── Applications (Pending, approved, denied applications)
├── Documents (Membership verification docs, by year)
├── Payments (Payment records, receipts, bank statements)
├── Reservations (Facility bookings, guest lists, approvals)
├── Audit & Logs (System activity, approvals, changes)
└── Archives (Inactive members, historical records)
## 4. Data Access & Permissions
Data access is limited to authorized users on a need-to-know basis.
### Who Can Access GEA Data
Board Members: Full access to all GEA data (membership, payments, reservations, approvals)
Treasurer: Access to payment data, member directory, household records, and audit logs
RSO: Access to verification documents (employment, ID, etc.) during application review
Members: Access to their own profile and reservation data only
Non-members: No access
### Member Directory
Active members can view basic directory of other active members
Directory includes: Name, email, phone (optional), household info
Directory does NOT include: ID numbers, passport info, employment docs, payment history
Members can opt out of directory listing (privacy setting in profile)
## 5. Data Protection & Security
GEA implements security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, loss, or corruption. See GEA Security & Privacy Policy for detailed security protocols.
### Encryption & Backups
All data in Google Workspace is encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (Google-managed encryption)
Google Workspace automatically backs up Shared Drive data
Sensitive documents (IDs, passports) stored in access-restricted folders
Treasurer maintains separate encrypted backup of payment records
### Data Breach & Loss Prevention
No downloading of sensitive data to personal devices (unless encrypted and approved)
No sharing of member data with third parties without Board approval
No forwarding of documents via unsecured email (use Google Drive links)
If breach suspected: Treasurer notifies Board immediately; GEA Security & Privacy Policy incident procedures followed
## 6. Data Retention Schedule
Data is retained according to legal requirements and operational needs. See Document Submission Policy for document-specific retention.
Active member records: Retained for duration of membership
Payment records: Retained for minimum 7 years (financial audit requirements)
Verification documents: Retained for minimum 3 years after membership termination
Audit/approval logs: Retained indefinitely (regulatory compliance, 6 FAM 500-546)
Reservation history: Retained for minimum 2 years
Denied applications: Retained for minimum 1 year
## 7. Data Minimization & Privacy
GEA collects only the minimum data necessary to manage membership and operations.
Members are asked for only required information during application
Optional fields are clearly marked
Members can update or correct their information at any time
Members can request deletion of data after membership termination (subject to legal/audit holds)
GEA does NOT collect: Social media profiles, employer performance reviews, personal medical info, criminal records
