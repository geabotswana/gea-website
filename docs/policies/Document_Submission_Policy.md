# **GEA DOCUMENT SUBMISSION POLICY**
Gaborone Employee Association
**Last Updated:** March 3, 2026
Effective: March 3, 2026
## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes which documents are required for each GEA membership category, how documents are verified, how long they are retained, and member privacy protections. A two-tier verification process (RSO review → GEA treasurer verification) ensures accuracy and security.
## 2. Document Submission During Application
All applicants submit required documents through the GEA member portal during the membership application process.
### Documents Required for ALL Applicants
Valid government-issued photo ID:
Botswana Omang (national ID), OR
Valid passport (any country), OR
Other government-issued ID with photo and expiration date
Passport-style color photo (2" x 2", digital upload)
Completed membership application form (portal form)
### Additional Documents by Membership Category

| Category | Required Documents | Verified By |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Full | Proof of USG employment (pay stub, employment letter, embassy ID badge, or official personnel file) | RSO (verifies employment) + Treasurer (final review) |
| Associate | Proof of employment (contract, job letter, company verification letter, or employment verification from HR) | RSO (verifies employer eligibility) + Treasurer (final review) |
| Affiliate | Proof of embassy employment (embassy employment verification letter, staff roster, or HR confirmation) | RSO (verifies embassy employment) + Treasurer (final review) |
| Diplomatic | Proof of diplomatic status (diplomatic passport, accreditation letter, or intl organization employment letter) | RSO (verifies diplomatic status) + Treasurer (final review) |
| Temporary | Proof of TDY or official visitor status (TDY orders, assignment letter, or official visitor designation) | RSO (verifies status) + Treasurer (final review) |
| Community/Guest | Proof of identity (passport or national ID); Board may request additional docs on case-by-case basis | RSO (verifies identity) + Board (case-by-case) + Treasurer (final review) |

## 3. Two-Tier Verification Process
All documents follow a two-stage verification process before membership is activated:
### Stage 1: RSO Document Review
RSO receives documents from GEA applicant portal
Verifies authenticity and legality of employment/status documentation
Checks for duplicate applications or conflicts
May request clarification or additional documentation
Marks documents as 'Verified' or requests resubmission
Timeline: 5 business days
### Stage 2: Treasurer Final Review
Treasurer receives RSO-verified documents
Confirms completeness: all required docs present and legible
Matches applicant identity across all documents
Files documents in secure storage
Approves membership activation
Timeline: 2 business days
## 4. Document Retention Policy
Documents are retained securely by GEA treasurer to support membership verification and regulatory compliance.
### Retention Period
Active member: Retained for duration of membership
Inactive/resigned member: Retained for minimum 3 years after membership termination
Application denied: Retained for minimum 1 year
Purpose: Audit trail, regulatory compliance (6 FAM 500-546), dispute resolution
### Storage & Security
Documents stored in GEA's secure storage system (Google Drive/Shared Drive)
Access restricted to: Treasurer and Board (need-to-know basis)
RSO does NOT retain copies; returns to GEA after verification
Digital documents follow GEA Data Management Policy for encryption and backup
### Document Destruction
After retention period expires, documents are securely deleted
Treasurer maintains deletion log (date, member name, documents destroyed)
Board reviews destruction log quarterly
## 5. Member Privacy & Data Protection
GEA protects member documents according to GEA Data Management Policy and applicable privacy regulations.
### What Members Need to Know
Documents are retained for membership verification only
Access is restricted to Treasurer and Board (need-to-know)
Documents are NOT shared with third parties without member consent
Documents follow GEA's Data Management & Security & Privacy policies
Members can request confirmation of retention and scheduled destruction date
Members can request document destruction (subject to legal/audit holds)
## 6. Incomplete or Unacceptable Documents
If documents are incomplete, illegible, or do not meet requirements, applicant is notified and given opportunity to resubmit.
RSO identifies issues and returns documents with specific rejection reason
Applicant has [X] business days to resubmit corrected documents
If applicant does not resubmit by deadline, application is marked as incomplete and may be archived
Applicant can reopen application by resubmitting documents
