# **GEA GUEST LIST POLICY**
Gaborone Employee Association
**Last Updated:** March 3, 2026
Effective: March 3, 2026
## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes requirements for GEA members hosting non-member guests at GEA facilities. Guest lists are required for Embassy security screening and RSO coordination. Clear identification standards ensure that guests can be properly acknowledged and that Embassy security protocols are met.
## 2. Guest Definition
A guest is defined as:
Any non-member individual attending a GEA facility event
Any individual not listed as a household member in the GEA membership directory
Includes: friends, extended family, colleagues, domestic staff, service providers, or other visitors
## 3. Guest Information Requirements
All guest information must be provided in full and legible format. Incomplete guest lists will be rejected by RSO and returned to the member for correction.
### Required Information for ALL Guests
FULL FIRST NAME (not initials, nicknames, or relationship names)
FULL LAST NAME (not initials, nicknames, or relationship names)
Date of Birth (MM/DD/YYYY format)
Relationship to household member (optional but helpful for RSO context)
### STRONGLY RECOMMENDED for Adults (18+)
Government-issued photo ID number (Botswana Omang, passport number, or other valid ID)
Country of ID issuance (especially if not Botswana)
Note: ID numbers assist RSO in security screening and prevent identity confusion
### For Children (Under 18)
Full first and last name (required)
Date of birth (required)
Relationship to household member (required; e.g., 'Friend's child', 'Cousin', 'Neighbor')
ID number (optional; not required for minors)
## 4. Unacceptable Guest Information Formats
The following formats are NOT acceptable and will result in RSO rejection:
Single names or nicknames only (e.g., 'Daniel', 'Maria', 'Blessing')
Relationship names instead of person's actual name (e.g., 'Mama Raney', 'Papa', 'Sister', 'Uncle')
Initials only (e.g., 'J.K.', 'R.M.')
Profession or role only (e.g., 'Nanny', 'House Helper', 'Driver')
Incomplete or illegible entries
## 5. Guest List Submission Process
Guest lists are submitted through the GEA member portal during facility reservation.
### Timing
Guest list deadline: [X] business days before the event
Members can update guest lists until deadline
One business day before deadline: Members receive final reminder email
### Portal Form Features
Dynamic form with add/remove guest rows (+/- buttons)
Form updates without page refresh; previously entered rows preserved
Fields: First Name, Last Name, Date of Birth, Relationship (optional), ID Number (optional)
Validation: Warns if guest name appears incomplete (only first name, only last name, or special characters only)
Submit button to finalize guest list
## 6. RSO Review & Guest Rejection
Embassy RSO reviews all guest lists for completeness and security clearance.
### RSO Actions
Approve guests (acknowledge all entries as acceptable)
Reject entire guest list if incomplete or unacceptable format
Reject individual guests with specific security concerns
Request clarification if information is ambiguous or illegible
### Incomplete Guest List Rejection
If guest list is rejected for format/completeness issues (e.g., missing last names, relationship names used as actual names, missing dates of birth), RSO will:
Return guest list to member with specific rejection reason
Specify which guest entries are unacceptable and why
Provide a new deadline for resubmission (typically 2-3 business days if close to event)
Note: If event is imminent and no resubmission time remains, RSO may reject the guest list and inform member that guests cannot be accommodated
### Individual Guest Rejection
RSO can reject specific guests for security or other official reasons
Member is notified of rejection with reason
Member can remove rejected guest or attempt resubmission with updated information
Remaining approved guests may proceed with the event
## 7. Final Guest List & Facility Staff
Once RSO approves the guest list, a final list is generated for facility staff:
Approved guests: [FIRST_NAME] [LAST_NAME]
Rejected guests (if any): [FIRST_NAME] [LAST_NAME] – REJECTED [reason]
Available to member in member portal
Available to facility managers for reference on event day
## 8. No-Guest Events
If a member indicates no guests (guest_count = 0) and submits no guest list entries:
No guest list submission required
Reservation proceeds without RSO review
Event marked as: 'Approved – No Guests'
