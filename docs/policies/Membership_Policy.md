# **GEA MEMBERSHIP POLICY**
*Gaborone Employee Association*
Approved: March 3, 2026 (Revised)

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes membership categories, eligibility requirements, sponsorship rules, dues, voting rights, and member privileges. The policy implements federal regulations 6 FAM 500-546 and the GEA By-Laws approved May 22, 2025.
## 2. Membership Categories
GEA offers six membership categories. Each category has different eligibility, sponsorship, dues, and access rights:
**Full Members: **U.S. Government direct-hire employees and their household members
**Associate Members: **U.S. Government-funded employees and contractors (minimum 51% USG funding), and their household members
**Affiliate Members: **Embassy locally-hired employees and their household members
**Diplomatic Members: **Personnel with official diplomatic or international organization status and their household members
**Temporary Members: **U.S. Government employees on temporary duty (TDY) or temporary assignment (maximum 6 months)
**Community/Guest Members: **Non-government individuals who are part of the local community and sponsored by a Full member

## 3. Membership Eligibility
Eligibility for each category is determined by employment status or official capacity:
**Full Members: **Direct U.S. Government employment; verified by employment letter, pay stub, or embassy ID
**Associate Members: **Minimum 51% U.S. Government funding or employment (contractor, grantee); verified by employment letter or contract. Applicants with less than 51% USG funding must apply as Community members.
**Affiliate Members: **Embassy employment (any status); verified by embassy employment verification letter
**Diplomatic Members: **Official diplomatic status or international organization personnel; verified by diplomatic passport or accreditation letter
**Temporary Members: **TDY or temporary assignment status; verified by official TDY orders or assignment letter
**Community/Guest Members: **No employment requirement; sponsored by active Full member; verified through sponsorship and identity documents

## 4. Sponsorship Requirements
Sponsorship confirms that an applicant is known to the community and in good standing.
### Sponsorship Table:
**Full Members: **NO sponsor required
**Associate Members: **NO sponsor required
**Affiliate Members: **NO sponsor required
**Diplomatic Members: **NO sponsor required
**Temporary Members: **NO sponsor required
**Community/Guest Members: **YES – must be sponsored by an active Full member

Sponsor Liability: Sponsors have NO financial or legal liability. Sponsorship is a community introduction and good-standing confirmation only. Sponsors have no ongoing obligations after the application is processed.

## 5. Dues & Payment
Annual membership dues (August 1 – July 31):
**Full: **$50 individual / $100 family
**Associate: **$50 individual / $100 family
**Affiliate: **$50 individual / $100 family
**Diplomatic: **$75 individual / $150 family
**Community/Guest: **$75 individual / $150 family
**Temporary: **$20 for period (6-month maximum)

Membership year: August 1 – July 31. Dues due by August 31. Facility access suspended if unpaid by deadline. Mid-year joiners: Pro-rated by quarter. Treasurer verifies payment before account activation.

## 6. Membership Rights
### Voting Rights:
Full members age 16+ only. All other categories do not have voting rights.
### Board Eligibility:
Full members age 16+ only. All other categories are not eligible for Board positions.
### Facility Access:
**Full & Associate Members: **Full access to all facilities (Tennis/Basketball, Leobo, Gym, Playground)
**Community/Guest Members: **Facility access at Board discretion (subject to supervision requirements per federal regulations)
**Affiliate, Diplomatic, Temporary Members: **Facility access to be determined by Board

### Guest Hosting:
All membership categories may invite guests to facilities. Guest hosting capability is configurable by the Board and may vary by membership category. See GEA Guest List Policy and Reservation Policy for guest requirements and procedures.
