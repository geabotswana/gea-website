# **GEA FACILITY RESERVATION POLICY**
Gaborone Employee Association
**Last Updated:** March 3, 2026
Effective: March 3, 2026
## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes how GEA members reserve and use shared facilities, including booking procedures, approval workflows, household booking limits, and guest management. The policy ensures fair access to facilities while maintaining Embassy security and operational efficiency.
## 2. Facilities & Reservation Types
GEA manages reservations for the following facilities:

| Facility | Type | Max Duration/Session | Household Limit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tennis Court / Basketball Court | Reservable | 2 hours | 3 hours/week |
| Leobo (Covered Meeting Area) | Reservable | 6 hours | 1 booking/month |
| Whole Facility | Reservable | [TBD] | [TBD] |
| Gym | Walk-up only | No limit | N/A |
| Children's Playground | Walk-up only | No limit | N/A |

## 3. Booking Procedures
Members reserve facilities through the GEA member portal (online booking system).
### Time Slots
Slots available at 15-minute intervals (:00, :15, :30, :45)
Minimum booking: 15 minutes
Maximum booking: 2 hours for Tennis/Basketball; 6 hours for Leobo
Example: 14:00–15:30 = 1.5-hour booking
### Household Booking Limits
Tennis/Basketball: 3 hours per calendar week (Sunday–Saturday)
Leobo: 1 booking per calendar month (up to 6 hours per booking)
Gym & Playground: No limits (walk-up access)
## 4. Approval Workflow
Approval requirements vary by facility and booking type. Below outlines the approval routing:
### Tennis / Basketball (Regular Booking)
If household usage + this booking ≤ 3 hours/week:
AUTO-APPROVED (no board review needed) ✓
### Tennis / Basketball (Excess Booking)
If household usage + this booking > 3 hours/week:
Marked as EXCESS BOOKING
Requires: Board approval (board@geabotswana.org)
Member can still see booking (status = 'Pending Board Approval')
Timeline: 1-day bumping window (see Section 5)
### Leobo (All Bookings)
All Leobo bookings require TWO-STAGE approval:
Stage 1: Management approval (mgt-notify@geabotswana.org)
Stage 2: Board approval (board@geabotswana.org) – after Mgmt approves
Timeline: 5 business days bumping window (see Section 5)
### Whole Facility (All Bookings)
All Whole Facility bookings require TWO-STAGE approval (same as Leobo)
Blocks both Tennis/Basketball and Leobo during reserved time
Board receives historical Whole Facility request data to identify patterns
Timeline: 5 business days bumping window
## 5. Bumping & Excess Booking Protocols
Excess bookings (those exceeding household limits) are allowed but may be 'bumped' if a regular booking is cancelled during the bump window. This ensures fair use of popular facilities.
### Bump Windows
Tennis/Basketball excess: 1 day before event
Leobo/Whole Facility: 5 business days before event
After bump window expires: Excess booking auto-confirms (locked in)
### How Bumping Works
If regular booking cancelled during bump window:
Excess booking automatically promoted to confirmed
Member notified: 'Your excess booking has been promoted to confirmed'
Calendar event updated
## 6. Guest Management
Members may host non-member guests at facilities. All guests require Embassy RSO acknowledgment for security clearance.
### Guest List Deadline
Guest list must be submitted [X] business days before event
Member specifies number of guests and provides: first name, last name, date of birth, relationship
Members can update guest list until deadline
### RSO Review
RSO (rso-notify@geabotswana.org) reviews guest list
Can approve or reject individual guests with reason
Member notified if guests rejected; can attempt resubmission
Final guest list provided to facility staff on event day
## 7. Cancellations & Modifications
Members can cancel or modify reservations through portal
Cancellation triggers: Waitlist promotion (if applicable), Board notification
Modification (change date/time): Treated as cancel + new booking request
Guest list deadline recalculates on modification
