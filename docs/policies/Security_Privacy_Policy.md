# **GEA SECURITY & PRIVACY POLICY**
Gaborone Employee Association
Effective: March 3, 2026
## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes GEA's security and privacy standards to protect member data, system integrity, and operational continuity. The policy covers data protection, access control, password requirements, incident response, and member privacy rights.
## 2. Data Protection
### Encryption
All data in Google Workspace encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest (Google-managed encryption)
Sensitive documents (passports, IDs, employment docs) stored in access-restricted folders
Payment records encrypted and backed up by Treasurer
No sensitive data downloaded to personal devices unless encrypted
### Backups & Disaster Recovery
Google Workspace Shared Drive automatically backed up by Google
Treasurer maintains separate encrypted backup of payment records
Backups tested quarterly to verify data integrity
Recovery time objective (RTO): 24 hours for critical data
## 3. Access Control
### User Authentication
All users authenticate with Google Workspace credentials
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) strongly recommended for Board and Treasurer
Passwords must meet Google's complexity requirements
Passwords should be changed every 90 days
### Role-Based Access
Board: Full access to all GEA data
Treasurer: Access to payments, member directory, household records, audit logs
Members: Access to own profile and reservation data only
RSO: Access to verification documents during application review only
Access removed immediately upon role change or membership termination
### Shared Accounts Prohibited
Treasurer email (treasurer@geabotswana.org) is a distribution list, not a login
Board email (board@geabotswana.org) is a distribution list, not a login
All users log in with individual Google Workspace accounts
Shared accounts make audit trails unreliable; individual accounts required
## 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties
Member data NOT shared with third parties without Board approval
RSO receives only necessary documents during application review
Bank account information (Absa, PayPal, Federal Credit Union) handled by Treasurer only
No forwarding of sensitive documents via email
All document sharing via secure Google Drive links
## 5. Incident Response
### Data Breach or Security Incident
Any suspected data breach or security incident reported immediately to Board
Treasurer or affected person notifies board@geabotswana.org
Board convenes emergency meeting within 24 hours
Response includes: Containment, assessment, notification, remediation
Incident documented and retained in audit logs
### Unauthorized Access
If unauthorized access suspected: Change affected passwords immediately
Review access logs to determine what was accessed
Notify affected members if their data accessed
Document incident and response in audit logs
## 6. Member Privacy Rights
Members can request what data GEA holds about them
Members can request correction of inaccurate data
Members can opt out of directory listing
Members can request data deletion after membership termination (subject to legal/audit holds)
Request submitted to Treasurer; Board reviews and responds within 10 business days
## 7. Policy Compliance & Review
All Board members and Treasurer trained on security and privacy practices
Policy reviewed annually and updated as needed
Violations reported to Board for disciplinary action
Serious violations (unauthorized data access) may result in membership termination
