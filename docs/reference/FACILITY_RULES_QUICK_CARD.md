**Capacity:** TBD

### Recreation Center - Leobo (Covered Meeting Area)

**Location:** Presidents Drive plot

**Description:** Thatch-roofed covered meeting area with built-in seating/braai facilities

**Capacity:** TBD

**Reservation Required?** YES – all bookings require approval

**Max Duration per Session:** 6 hours per booking

**Household Booking Limit:** 1 booking per calendar month

**Approval Routing:** Two-stage approval required
1. Management approval (mgt-notify@geabotswana.org)
2. Board approval (board@geabotswana.org) – after Mgmt approves

**Bumping Window:** 5 business days before event (excess bookings auto-confirm after this)

**Guest List:** REQUIRED – submit [X] business days before event

**Equipment:** Charcoal/wood-fueled braai and barbecues

**Special Notes:**
- All bookings (regular and excess) require both Mgmt and Board approval
- Cannot overlap with Tennis/Basketball or Whole Facility bookings
- Usage tracked monthly; resets 1st of month 2:00 AM GMT+2
- Popular facility; plan ahead

## Violations & Consequences

Members who violate facility rules may face:
- Warning (first offense)
- Temporary facility access suspension (repeated violations)
- Permanent facility access revocation (severe violations or safety concerns)
- Disciplinary action by GEA Board

Examples of violations:
- Unauthorized use outside operating hours
- Bringing guests without proper approval (Leobo)
- Damaging facility equipment
- Disruptive behavior
- Failure to respect member reservations
