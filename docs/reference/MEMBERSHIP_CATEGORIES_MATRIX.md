## Overview

GEA has six membership categories determined by a sequential eligibility questionnaire. Every applicant receives exactly ONE category based on employment status.

---

## Full Membership

**Eligibility:** Direct-hire USG employees; PSCs recruited from outside Botswana

**Sponsorship:** Not required

**Annual Dues:** $50 individual / $100 family

**Rights:** Full voting (age 16+), Board eligibility, full facility access, guest hosting

**Regulatory Basis:** 6 FAM 542(a), (b), 538

**Application:** ~10 days (3 board + 5 RSO + 2 treasurer)

---

## Associate Membership

**Eligibility:** Minimum 51% U.S. Government funding or employment (contractors, federally-funded program staff, embassy employees recruited from outside Botswana). Applicants with less than 51% USG funding do not qualify for Associate status and must apply as Community members.

**Sponsorship:** Not required

**Annual Dues:** $50 individual / $100 family

**Rights:** Full facility access, guest hosting; NO voting or Board

**Regulatory Basis:** 6 FAM 542(b); By-Laws §7(B)

**Application:** ~10 days

---

## Affiliate Membership

**Eligibility:** Host country nationals; TCNs recruited locally in Botswana

**Sponsorship:** Not required

**Annual Dues:** $50 individual / $100 family

**Rights:** Specified facilities (Board discretion); NO voting, Board, or guaranteed facility access

**Regulatory Basis:** 6 FAM 544(a); By-Laws §7(C)

**Application:** ~10 days

---

## Diplomatic Membership

**Eligibility:** Registered diplomats; international organization staff

**Sponsorship:** Not required

**Annual Dues:** $75 individual / $150 family

**Rights:** Specified facilities (Board discretion); NO voting or Board

**Regulatory Basis:** 6 FAM 545; By-Laws §7(D)

**Application:** ~10 days

---

## Temporary Membership

**Eligibility:** USG employees on TDY or official visitor status (max 6 months)

**Sponsorship:** Not required

**Annual Dues:** $20 for 6-month period (pro-rated)

**Rights:** Specified facilities (Board discretion); NO voting, Board, or guest hosting

**Regulatory Basis:** 6 FAM 542(a); By-Laws §7(E)

**Special:** Auto-expires at 6 months; not renewable

**Application:** ~1-2 days (no document review)

---

## Community/Guest Membership

**Eligibility:** Local nationals; non-USG-affiliated visitors; Board approval (case-by-case)

**Sponsorship:** Yes – must be Full member (active, paid)

**Annual Dues:** $75 individual / $150 family

**Rights:** Specified facilities (Board discretion, excluding duty-free); NO voting or Board

**Regulatory Basis:** By-Laws §7(F); federal basis PENDING clarification

**Status:** Assumed ACTIVE for documentation; See regulatory analysis for gaps

**Application:** ~15-20 days (Board case-by-case approval)

---

## Membership Year & Dues

**Membership Year:** August 1 – July 31

**Dues Deadline:** August 31 (for current membership year)

**Pro-rating:** By quarter (mid-year joiners pay proportional amount)

**Example:** Joining in November (Q2 = Oct-Dec) → Pay 75% of annual dues for remainder of year

**Facility Access:** Suspended if dues not received by August 31

---

## Sponsorship

**Who needs sponsor:**
- Associate, Affiliate, Diplomatic, Community/Guest: YES (Full member only)
- Full, Temporary: NO

**Sponsor responsibility:**
- Verification only (confirms applicant known and in good standing)
- NO financial or legal liability
- NO responsibility for member behavior
- Can revoke if no longer confident in good standing
